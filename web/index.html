ABOUT

Pure Pressure is the visionary behind Muesli of Mussels, an experimental music and art collective pushing boundaries in sound design and digital creativity.

Drawing inspiration from minimalist aesthetics and complex audio landscapes, Pure Pressure crafts immersive sonic experiences that challenge conventional listening practices.

Based in the intersection of technology and art, Muesli of Mussels explores the boundaries between music, sound design, and experimental audio.
